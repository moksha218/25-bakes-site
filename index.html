Hey there! I’m a 25-year-old finance professional by day and a passionate baker by... well, anytime I can get my hands on flour and frosting! What started as a weekend stress-buster quickly turned into a full-blown love affair with baking — the kind that smells like warm cookies and late-night cake experiments. This little bakery is my sweet escape from spreadsheets and numbers. Every dessert I make is handcrafted with love, lots of butter, and just the right amount of chaos. Whether it's a classic chocolate cake or a quirky cupcake flavor, I believe that every bite should feel like a warm hug. Thanks for being here and supporting this sugar-filled side hustle. Let’s make life a little sweeter, one bite at a time.
